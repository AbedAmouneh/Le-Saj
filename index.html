Our Story

A Passion for Authentic Food

Founded in 2017 by three food-loving friends — Mohamad, Rochelle, and Adam — Les Saj started with a simple mission: bring the real flavours of the Middle East to Winnipeg. Every dish is prepared fresh daily using traditional recipes and the finest ingredients.

The name "Les Saj" is inspired by the iconic dome-shaped griddle used across the Middle East to bake our signature flatbread — the heart of every wrap we serve.
